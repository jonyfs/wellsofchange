Our Story

Wells of Change was founded in 2015 by a group of passionate individuals who witnessed firsthand the devastating effects of water scarcity during humanitarian missions in developing regions. What began as a small initiative to build a single well in a rural village has grown into a global organization dedicated to creating sustainable water solutions worldwide.

Our founders recognized that access to clean water is not just about survivalâ€”it's the foundation for education, economic development, and community health. With this understanding, Wells of Change was established with a mission to implement holistic water solutions that empower communities to thrive.

Over the years, we've expanded our approach to include education, sanitation programs, and agricultural initiatives that help communities maximize the benefits of reliable water access. We believe in working collaboratively with local leaders and experts to ensure our projects are culturally appropriate and sustainable for generations to come.

Our Vision

A world where every person has access to clean, safe water as a fundamental right, enabling communities to thrive and reach their full potential.

Our Mission

To develop and implement sustainable water solutions in partnership with communities, while educating and inspiring global action on water issues.
