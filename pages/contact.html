Frequently Asked Questions

How are donations used?

expand_more

We allocate approximately 85% of all donations directly to our field programs, which include well construction, sanitation education, and agricultural support. The remaining 15% covers essential administrative costs and fundraising efforts to expand our reach. Our annual reports provide detailed financial breakdowns.

How can I volunteer if I don't have specialized skills?

expand_more

We welcome volunteers with all skill sets! Many of our volunteer opportunities don't require specialized knowledge. You can help with event planning, administrative tasks, community outreach, or social media. We also provide training for certain field positions.

How does Wells of Change ensure project sustainability?

expand_more

Sustainability is central to our approach. We work closely with communities to ensure local ownership of each project. We provide training in maintenance and repair, establish local water committees, and implement usage fee systems that fund ongoing maintenance. We also conduct regular follow-up visits to monitor projects.

Can I specify which project my donation supports?

expand_more

Yes! When making a donation, you can designate your gift to support a specific program area or region. If you're interested in funding a particular project, please contact our development team to discuss options for restricted giving.

How can I organize a fundraiser for Wells of Change?

expand_more

We'd love to help you organize a fundraiser! You can use our online platform to create a personal fundraising page, or contact our team for support with larger events. We provide resources such as logos, fact sheets, and presentation materials to help make your fundraiser a success.
